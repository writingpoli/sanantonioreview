Support

The San Antonio Review is a costly endeavor undertaken with love by its editors and publisher. It is not a profit-seeking enterprise. It aims to herald interesting voices in the arts. It receives no financial support beyond donations, submission and Amazon commission fees, which are used to recover some of the on-going costs of web hosting, printing, Submittable and other infrastructure. Check out our finances (such as they are) at Open Collective.
